About the Library

Baba Sawanth Nath Library is a community-run library in Dalanwas, Haryana, dedicated to empowering students for competitive exams and fostering knowledge. The library houses a wide variety of books including engineering, medical, methodological, and competition preparation books. Time to time, the library organizes events like exams, blood donation camps, speech competitions, yoga sessions, and cleanliness drives.
