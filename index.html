About the Village

Dalanwas (or Dalenwas) is a large village in Mahendragarh district of Haryana. As per the Census 2011, the total population is approximately 3,672 with about 702 families. The village has a literacy rate of 76.65%, higher than the state average, with 89.96% male literacy and 62.18% female literacy. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
